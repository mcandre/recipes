# REQUIREMENTS

* stove
* stove pot
* frying pan
* olive oil
* colander
* mixing bowl
* spaghetti stirrer
* stirring spoon
* serving bowls
* serving spoons
* tri-color rotini
* ground turkey
* button mushrooms
* yellow onion
* parmesean cheese
* salt

# INSTRUCTIONS

1. Fry turkey in olive oil.
2. Begin boiling rotini with a pinch of salt.
3. Dice onion.
5. Drain rotini.
6. Mix rotini, turkey, mushrooms, onion, cheese, another pinch of salt.
