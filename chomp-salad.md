# CHOMP SALAD

# REQUIREMENTS

• chef's knife
• cutting board
• mixing bowl
• tinfoil
• mixing spoon
• serving bowl
• serving fork
• smoked salmon
• 1 head lettuce, chopped
• 1 carrot, peeled
• 1/2 red onion, julienned
• 1/2 cucumber, sliced
• 1 tomato, sliced
• button mushrooms, sliced
• sesame ginger salad dressing

# INSTRUCTIONS

1. Mix vegetables.
2. Serve with sesame ginger dressing.
3. Refrigerate leftovers.
