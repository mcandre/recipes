# ICE TEA

# REQUIREMENTS

* refrigerator
* stove
* stove pot
* pitcher
* measuring cup
* stirring spoon
* 8 cups water
* 6 small bags black tea leaves
* 1/4 cup sugar
* 1/2 lemon, sliced
* ice
* cups

# INSTRUCTIONS

1. Boil 4 cups water in pot.
2. Remove pot from heat.
3. Steep tea bags 10 minutes.
4. Discard tea bags.
5. Stir sugar until dissolved.
6. Add lemon.
7. Refrigerate 1 hour.
8. Serve over ice.
