# Chomp Salad

Serves 3.

## REQUIREMENTS

* chef's knife
* vegetable peeler
* Mixing bowl
* Stirring spoon
* Serving bowls
* Iceberg lettuce
* cherry tomatoes
* button mushrooms
* red onion
* carrots
* sesame ginger salad dressing

# Preparation

1. Chop iceberg lettuce, onion.
2. Peel carrots.
3. Stir vegetables together.
4. Lightly drizzle dressing.
5. Mix, serve.
