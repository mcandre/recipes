# Summary

lemonade pour les enfants

# Requirements

* 6 cups water
* 10 lemons
* 1 cup sugar
* 9 maraschino cherries
* pitcher
* stirring utensil
* refrigerator
* ice

# Instructions

1. Add water to pitcher.
2. Squeeze 2 cups juice from 10 lemons into pitcher.
3. Stir in sugar.
4. Add cherries.
5. Refrigerate for 3 hours.
6. Serve with ice.
